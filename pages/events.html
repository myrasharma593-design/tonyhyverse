The Firewalk

"If you can walk on fire, what else can you do?"

1. The Metaphor

The firewalk is not about the fire. It's about what it takes to get yourself to take the first step. It proves that your fear is a liar.

2. State of Mind

To walk across 1200-degree coals without burning, you must be in a peak state of certainty. Doubt will burn you.

3. The Breakthrough

Once you cross the coals, you realize that the impossible is possible. You take this new belief into your career, relationships, and health.
