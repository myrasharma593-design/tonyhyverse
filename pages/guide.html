Total Immersion Learning

Why 50 minutes is not enough. To change your life, you must immerse yourself completely.

1. Why Immersion?

If you want to learn a language, you move to the country. If you want to change your life, you don't do it in 30-minute sessions once a week. You immerse.

2. The Seminar Environment

  • 12 to 15 hour days.
  • Controlled temperature (kept cool to keep you alert).
  • Dynamic lighting and sound.
  • Active participation to retain 90% of information.

3. Breaking Patterns

Immersion fatigues the conscious mind, allowing the subconscious to be reprogrammed. This is where true transformation happens.
