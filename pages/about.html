About Tony Robbins

Entrepreneur, #1 NY Times Bestselling Author, Philanthropist, and the Nation’s #1 Life & Business Strategist.

  • Practical Psychology: Strategies that work immediately.

Tony has empowered more than 50 million people from 100 countries through his audio, video, and life training programs.

A world where people live with passion, purpose, and contribution.
