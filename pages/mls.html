Neuro-Linguistic Programming (NLP)

The study of the structure of subjective experience. Learn how to model success and reprogram your mind.

1. What is NLP?

NLP is a psychological approach that involves analyzing strategies used by successful individuals and applying them to reach a personal goal.

2. Reframing

Changing the meaning of an event by changing the context or content. "It's not a failure, it's feedback."

3. Anchoring

Creating a physical trigger for a powerful emotional state. Tony often uses gestures or sounds to anchor peak states.

4. Modeling

The fastest way to master any skill is to find someone who has already achieved the result you want and model their beliefs and physiology.
